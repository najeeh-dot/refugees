Our Impact: Changing Lives, One Step at a Time

Together, we’re creating real change

Education

Education is the key to breaking cycles of poverty. Your support helps us establish schools, provide learning materials, and empower refugee children with quality education. Over 50,000 children are already on the path to brighter futures—your contribution can help even more.

Shelter

Shelter is a fundamental need. Your support helps build safe, stable housing for refugee families, providing them with security, privacy, and hope. Over 10,000 families now have a place to call home because of donors like you.                                                                                                 

Healthcare

Access to medical care saves lives. By supporting healthcare initiatives, you provide refugees with vital services like medical checkups, vaccinations, and life-saving treatments. Over 80,000 individuals annually receive care in our health clinics, ensuring they can live healthy, dignified lives.
